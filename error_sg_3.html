Elementary band-representations with time-reversal symmetry of the Double Space Group P2 (No. 3)

The first row shows the Wyckoff position from which the band representation is induced.
In parentheses, the symbol of the point group isomorphic to the site-symmetry group.

The second row gives the symbol ρ↑G, where ρ is the irrep of the site-symmetry group.
In parentheses, the dimension of the representation.

The output shows the decomposition of the band representations into irreps of the little groups
of the given k-vectors in the first column.
In parentheses, the dimensions of the representations.
