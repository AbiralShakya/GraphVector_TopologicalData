The first row of the table shows the Wyckoff position from which the band co-representation is induced.
In parentheses, the symbol of the point group isomorphic to the site-symmetry magnetic group and its unitary subgroup.

The second row gives the symbol ρ↑G, where ρ is the irrep of the site-symmetry magnetic group.
In parentheses, the dimension of the co-representation.

The output shows the decomposition of the band co-representations into co-reps of the little groups of the given
